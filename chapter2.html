MISSION, VISION, AND GOALS

Note on Agency Profile: As a Private Law Practice, Villamar Law Office does not maintain a formalized or publicly posted Mission, Vision, and Goal statement, unlike public or corporate institutions. Instead, the firm operates based on the professional standards of the legal profession and the personal commitment of the lead counsel to provide quality legal services.

👁️

Vision

To be a trusted legal partner in Occidental Mindoro, recognized for integrity, professional competence, and the successful resolution of client legal concerns through personalized representation.

🎯

Mission

To provide personalized and effective legal services that protect the rights and interests of clients through diligent representation and sound legal advice tailored to the community's needs.

📌

Goals

  • To maintain a high standard of legal ethics in every case handled.
  • To provide accessible legal assistance to the local community.
  • To ensure efficient handling of administrative and legal documentation.
3

BACKGROUND OF THE AGENCY

The practice started as Rañon, Villamar, and Associates in 1996 with an office at M.H. Del Pilar St., San Jose, Occ. Mindoro. Retired Judge Antonio Rañon and former public prosecutor Atty. Francis T. Villamar served as senior partners.

Upon the demise of Judge Rañon, Atty. Francis T. Villamar became a solo practitioner under the name F.T. Villamar Law Office. In 2018, Atty. Lawrence P. Villamar assumed the practice, renaming the office as Villamar Law Office.
